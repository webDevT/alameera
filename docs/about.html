Vision

To be the leading manufacturer of Mediterranean food products in the region. While creating a performance based environment for our people to thrive in and maximize their potential.

Mission

To produce and supply a wide variety of Mediterranean food items while preserving the goodness of nature in each ingredient and maintaining the authentic taste of all products.

AL AMEERA FOODSTUFF INDUSTRIES was established in 2006 as a specialized manufacturer of Tahina and Halawa in the UAE.

Our roots

Supported by its product quality and high service standard, the factory has grown progressively, to become the largest manufacturer of Tahina and Halawa in the UAE and among the well reputed in the GCC. Since then, Al Ameera Foodstuff has expanded its operations to include more products in an organic development of the existing range that entwined with the company vision. Today, Al Ameera Range includes; Tahina, Halawa, Olive oil, and confectionary Spread.

Al Ameera is the only ISO 22000:2005 certified Tahina factory in the UAE..

Production in the factory is carried out in accordance to strict hygienic standards and by using advanced manufacturing techniques. As the production line is operated automatically, no human contact is required after raw material feeding stage and until the products are packed and ready to be shipped. During the manufacturing operation, Samples are collected from different production stages and then tested in our modern laboratory before the final product receives the approval to come out of the factory. All these steps are put there to ensure our clients a product of the highest quality.

Today, Al Ameera has a good presence in the local market backed by a strong distribution channel and delivery fleet. The brand is available in most of the modern and traditional trade with good consumer awareness. The products are also positioned as the first choice in the HORECA sector with strong distribution to many of the high end restaurants and 5 stars hotels. In addition, the factory contract manufactures many private label in the market which significantly expands the company market share in this product category.

Internationally, and with the partnership and support of our agents we are present in over 25 countries. Some of those countries are: Jordan, Morocco, United Kingdom, India, Australia.
