Tahina

Tahina is one of the most important ingredients in Mediterranean and middle-eastern cuisine. Made from pure ground sesame seeds, a natural and healthy source of protein, fat, and multiple other minerals, tahinais what gives many of the famous Arabic dishes, like Hummus, its mouthwatering creamy and rich taste.

Halawa

Halawa, a traditional and healthy confectionary, is made mainly from sesame seeds. This product packs an amazing amount of nutritional benefits. It’s a good source of calcium and magnesium and a healthy source of energy. It’s a perfect snack for kids in their growing age and main item on breakfast table.

Spread
